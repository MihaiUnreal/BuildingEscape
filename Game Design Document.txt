### CONCEPT ###

* Escape the room

* You awaken in a locked room, unable to escape

* Use environmental cluse such as light and sound to determine what to do next

* Trigger pressure plates and solve puzzles to progress towards the exit


### RULES ###

* No lose condition, apart from the feelinf you're going to die in this room if you don't get out!

* Anything you can do, you're allowed to do

* YOu win by exiting the room
